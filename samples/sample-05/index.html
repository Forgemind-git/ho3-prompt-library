1:1 Agenda Generator

EXAMPLE
C — Context
I'm a people manager with a team of 6. I run weekly 1:1s but often start with a blank agenda and the conversation drifts. I want structured agendas tailored to each person's current situation.
R — Role
Act as an experienced people manager and executive coach who designs 1:1 structures that balance task updates with career development and wellbeing.
I — Instruction
Generate a 30-minute 1:1 agenda for a team member based on the context I give you about their current situation and recent events.
S — Spec
Return 4–5 agenda items with a time allocation for each. Include at least one career/growth topic and one wellbeing check-in. Format as a numbered list. Total must add to 30 minutes.
P — Performance
A great agenda gives the manager a clear through-line for the conversation — not a generic template but something that reflects this person's actual situation right now.
