# HO3 Sample 4 — Support Team Prompt Library

## What you'll build
A ready-to-use library of 8 support prompts for a team that answers the same issues in wildly different tones. Each prompt is written in the **CRISP** format (Context, Role, Instruction, Spec, Performance) so Claude gives you warm, on-brand, consistent drafts — triage, replies, escalations, QA and more. Open the page, copy a prompt, paste it into Claude.ai, and you're done.

## Use it with your Claude.ai subscription
No API key needed. Just your normal Claude.ai login.

1. Open the file **`index.html`** from this folder in your web browser (double-click it).
2. You'll see 8 prompt cards (triage, replies, escalations, refunds and more). Pick the one you need.
3. Click the green **Copy prompt** button on that card.
4. Open **Claude.ai** in another tab and paste the prompt into the chat box.
5. Replace the `[PLACEHOLDERS]` (like `[CUSTOMER NAME]` or `[PASTE TICKET]`) with your real details, then press Enter.
6. Read Claude's draft, tweak it, and send. If the tone is off, tell Claude "warmer" or "more direct".

No API key needed.

## The prompts in this library
Every card is already filled in and ready to copy:

- **Ticket Triage & Priority** — categorise and prioritise an incoming ticket
- **First Reply Template** — a warm, helpful first response by issue type
- **Escalation Summary** — hand a complex case to a senior agent fast
- **Refund Decision Note** — a clear, fair refund decision with rationale
- **Knowledge Base Article** — turn a resolved ticket into a help-centre article
- **QA Review** — score a reply against your quality bar with feedback
- **Angry Customer De-escalation** — a calm, empathetic reply that lowers the heat
- **Weekly Support Summary** — surface themes and trends for your lead

## CRISP format reminder
Every prompt follows this five-part shape, which is why the outputs are so much better:

- **C — Context:** the background situation
- **R — Role:** who Claude should act as
- **I — Instruction:** the core task
- **S — Spec:** format, length, tone constraints
- **P — Performance:** what a great output looks like

## Make it your own
- Swap the `[PLACEHOLDERS]` for a real (anonymised) ticket from your queue.
- Add your brand voice and policies to the Spec so replies sound like your team.
- Copy a card's HTML block to add a prompt for your own flow, like a cancellation save.
